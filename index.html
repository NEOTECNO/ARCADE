What is ARCADE STORE ?

ARCADE STORE is a collection of games for your only enjoyment, hand made by our most talented devs working on Ethereum blockchain. They are unique digital videogames and no two of them are exactly the same.

Playing any of the games gives you a chance to earn TOKEN_NAME, and grants you access to holders-only benefits!

GAMES

DEATH'S CALLING

An spine-chilling yet thrilling play-to-earn survivor game! Dive into a world of supernatural survival and earn tokens passively as you outsmart the eerie challenges. With every heart-pounding victory, you'll accumulate tokens to redeem for valuable ETH rewards. Are you brave enough to answer Death's Calling and reap your chilling rewards?

Prepare to embark on a heart-pounding survival adventure, crafted in homage to the celebrated "20 Minutes Until Dawn" game, yet enhanced with our own distinct innovations. Here, you'll be armed with an arsenal of weapons and tantalized by the promise of upgrades, all while facing formidable challenges that test your wits and courage. Your journey isn't just about survival; it's also an opportunity to accumulate tokens passively, amplifying the stakes. Every passing minute amplifies the intensity, pushing your limits to the edge. Can you navigate the relentless challenges and emerge triumphant from the full 20-minute ordeal? The clock is ticking, and your destiny awaits.




FORGOTTEN SOULS

Forgotten Souls offers players an immersive play-to-earn interactive gaming experience, where they traverse diverse realms, confront enigmatic creatures, and strategically adapt to unique boss attacks, ensuring a compelling and dynamic gameplay journey.

Your ultimate objective is to survive and defeat all formidable bosses. Each boss, presents a distinctive set of attacks and strategies, requiring players to adapt dynamically to overcome these formidable adversaries. With unique abilities and varied modes of assault, our bosses promise a compelling and strategic gameplay experience, ensuring that players must continuously evolve their tactics for triumph.

Stay tuned for more updates as we chart this exciting course for the future of our games.
